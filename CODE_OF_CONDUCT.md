# Code of conduct

## Our pledge

We commit to making participation in this project harassment-free for everyone, regardless
of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and accept constructive feedback.
- Focus discussion on the code, behavior, and evidence.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Disruptive conduct that prevents productive collaboration.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to the repository maintainer at
mailto:stringke.me@gmail.com.

The maintainer will review reports, protect reporter privacy, and take proportionate action.
